## What is Historical Room Viewer?

This tool provides an overview of a searched user public room history, including details what outfits users are wearing in those rooms. It allows you to track user-owned rooms to observe their presence and activities, with data accessible in 1 hour intervals for the past 3 hours.

## Room Info

Room information includes details about each room, such as its description, ownership, capacity limit etc.

## Room Stats

Room stats provides a comprehensive overview of how searched rooms are performing on any specific date. It features a bar chart that displays the number of visitors throughout the day. Each bar represents the total visitor count for that specific hour, allowing both you and the room owner to easily identify peak activity times.
